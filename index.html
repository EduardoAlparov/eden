Behind Eden Garden Jewelry

Nature-inspired and guided by the magic of love, our family-owned, fine jewelry business specializes in engagement rings and wedding bands that capture the beauty of the supernatural, and its effervescent spirit. Eden Garden Jewelry provides a detail-orientated craftsmanship based in the East of Spain, with insured and fast worldwide shipping, so that your ring reflects your truest self, no matter where you are.

We believe that each ring at Eden Garden Jewelry embodies the alchemy that continues to bring people together in the age-old ritual of love.

IGI Laboratory Ring Certification: Quality and Authenticity

Backed by the renowned International Gemological Institute, IGI certification guarantees the quality, authenticity and value of your fine jewelry.

Your soul will be at ease and heart in the right place with a detailed report. You’ll have a complete biography of the ring of your choosing – detailed information on all the gemstones used, as well as the gold type and weight of the ring.
